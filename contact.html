M.I.C

Ms. Hirushi Dilpriya

The Mathematics & Statistics Circle of NSBM was founded in 2022 under the guidance of Ms. Hirushi Dilpriya, who continues to inspire students with her passion for numbers and logical thinking.

With a vision to cultivate analytical skills and a love for mathematics, she has played a pivotal role in shaping the club's journey, organizing engaging events, and fostering a thriving community of math enthusiasts.

Under her leadership, the club has grown into a space where students can explore pure mathematics, data science, and statistics while collaborating on exciting projects and competitions.
